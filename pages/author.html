About the author and administrator of CodeSync.org

by Sean Stafford

Hi, my name is Sean Stafford. I'm the author and owner of CodeSync.org.
I'm a full-time college student working towards a MS in Computer Science.

In my spare time I am a web developer and a game programmer/designer. I license all my code
either under full copyright or I copyleft it GPLv3 or AGPLv3. I believe anywhere in-between on
code is just indecisive or due to insecurity.

I generally support licensing of any of my graphics under full copyright, CC-BY-ND, CC-BY-SA, or the GPLv3 license.

In addition to this site, I'm the leader of team called the Alpha Trickster Project whose goal is to create a C++ 2D MMORPG engine and client meant to replace the game by NTREEV™ called Trickster Online™.

Neither client nor the server have reached alpha stage yet so there are no releases available.

As far as the CodeSync.org goes or if you would like to help with the Alpha Trickster Project,
you can contact me through my email: codesync@member.fsf.org

Thanks for stopping by the site.

~Sean Stafford
