About the author and administrator of CodeSync.org

by Sean Stafford

Hi, my name is Sean Stafford. I'm the author and owner of CodeSync.org.
I'm a full-time college student.

In my spare time I am a web developer and a game programmer/designer. I license all my code
either under full copyright or I copyleft it GPLv3 or AGPLv3.

I generally support licensing of any of my graphics under full copyright, CC-BY-ND, CC-BY-SA, or the GPLv3 license.

If you have questions about CodeSync.org, you can contact me through
my email: pyrosamurai@member.fsf.org

If you would like to add a game or tool to the lists, please send me a
pull request on github.

Thanks for stopping by the site.

~Sean Stafford
